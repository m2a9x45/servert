Customer Privacy Notice

Who we are?

We are Servert Limited and operate under the name Servert. We are registered with the UK Data Protection Authority (The Information Commissioner’s Office or ICO) under number ZA644527.

This notice will explain how and why we use your personal data. If you have any questions about this notice or wish to contact our Data Protection Officer (DPO) please email us

The information we hold about you

Details you provided when you registered your intreast. Like your name and email address.

Why we use your personal information

European Data Protection laws state we must have a lawful basis for using your personal data. At least one of the following must apply: contractual or legal duty, legitimate interest, public interest, vital individual interest or consent.

The lawful basis we use to process your data is both contractual and a legitimate interest.

Who we share your data with

Cloud computing power and storage providers like OVH. Email senders such as mailgun or sendgrid.

How long we keep your data

We keep your data for as long as you are using servert. In some cases we may keep your data longer to comply with the law.

Your rights

  • to access the personal data we hold about you, or to receive a copy of it
  • to ask for a copy of your personal data in a portable (machine-readable) format or have us send it to someone else
  • to make us correct inaccurate data
  • to ask us to delete, block or suppress your data, though for legal reasons we might not always be able to do it
  • to withdraw any consent you’ve given us
