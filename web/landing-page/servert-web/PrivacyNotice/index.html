Customer Privacy Notice

Who we are?

We're servert limited and operate under the name servert. We’re registered with the UK data protection authority (the Information Commissioner’s Office or ICO) under number ZA644527.

This notice will explain how and why we use your personal data. If you have any question about this notice or want to contact our Data Protection Officer (DPO) you can email us

The information we hold about you

Details you gave us when you register your interest like your name and email address.

Why we use your personal indormation

European Data protection laws say we need to have a lawful basis for using your personal data. At least one of the following must apply: contractual or legal duty, legitimate interest, public interest, vital individual interest or consent.

The lawful basis we use to process your data is both contractual and a legitimate interest.

Who we share your data with

Cloud computing power and storage providers like OVH. Email sender such as mailgun or sendgrid.

How long we keep your data

We keep your data for as long as you're using servert. In some cases we may keep your data longer to comply with the law.

Your rights

Your rights :

  • access the personal data we hold about you, or to get a copy of it
  • ask for a copy of your personal data in a portable (machine-readable) format or make us send it to someone else
  • make us correct inaccurate data
  • ask us to delete, 'block' or suppress your data, though for legal reasons we might not always be able to do it
  • withdraw any consent you’ve given us
